My Experience

Before I came to USA, I had lived in Shanghai since I was born. In 2015, I participated in the college entrance examination and was then admitted to University of Michigan - Shanghai Jiatong University Joint Institute (UM-SJTU JI).

At that time, I was very nervous to study there because the official language for this institute is English and I had never thought of studying everything in English. To make things worse, I had little background in my major - Electrical and Computer Engineering. Fortunately, I made a quick adaptation to the new environment.

In 2017 winter, I exchanged to University of Navarra for one month, studying Spanish. I would like to learn Spanish because Spanish is the second most spoken language in the world. Also, it was my first time to Europe, and it was a great opportunity for me to know about a different culture.

However, when I came back to China, I found that there was not much Spanish language course in Shanghai. In other words, it was hard for me to continue learning Spanish during the semesters in my university. Therefore, I chose German as my second major in SJTU and after a year of study, I successfully got a chance to exchange to Technical University of Berlin for one month in 2018 winter, studying 3D printing. In this case, not only could I practice my oral German ability with native speakers, but I was also able to experince the local customs. By the way, 3D printing was a very cool technology for me and the equipments there was more advanced than in SJTU.

After I came back from Berlin, I had a tough time finding an intern as well as preparing TOEFL and GRE tests for applications for graduate schools. I also decided my track in the future to be either data science or computer engineering.

At the end of April, it occurred to me that University of Michigan School of Information offered JI students a 3+2 MSI program that allowed us, junior students, to study data analytics/science there as graduate students.

This is why I am here, and where my new life begins.
